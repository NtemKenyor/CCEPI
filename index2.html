🎵 Music as a Tool for Peace & Opportunity

Established in 2008, CCEPI is a nonprofit organization using participatory music, education, and dialogue to empower youth and foster peaceful, inclusive communities across Nigeria.

🌍 About Us

The Centre for Community Empowerment and Peace Initiative (CCEPI) is a grassroots nonprofit dedicated to youth development, peacebuilding, and cultural education.

We believe that music is more than art — it is a universal language capable of healing divisions, creating livelihoods, and restoring hope.

🎼 Our Programs

Ongoing Program

CCEPI Music Fusion 🎹

A structured youth music training program offering hands-on instruction in percussion, keyboards, strings, and fretted instruments.

  • Weekly practical sessions
  • Mentorship & peer learning
  • Teaching & performance pathways
Mobile Program

Harmony for Peace 🕊️

A one-day musical peace dialogue program combining drumming, storytelling, and guided reflection to promote unity and healing.

  • Community music circles
  • Peace dialogue sessions
  • Adaptable, mobile delivery

📈 Our Impact

For over a decade, CCEPI has supported youth-focused initiatives in education, technology, peacebuilding, and cultural development.

Our work includes long-term skill-building programs, community workshops, and partnerships that expand opportunity for underserved populations.
