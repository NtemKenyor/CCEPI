About CCEPI

Where creativity meets community

The Centre for Community Empowerment and Peace Initiative (CCEPI) is a grassroots nonprofit based in Nigeria. Since 2008, we have worked at the heart of underserved communities — turning music into a vehicle for hope, healing, and opportunity.

We believe that a young person with a drum in their hands and a song in their heart is a young person empowered.

Our Programs

Transforming lives
through music

Two flagship programs, one mission: to place music at the center of youth development and community peacebuilding.

🎯 Ongoing Program
🎹

CCEPI Music Fusion

A structured, long-term music education program equipping 100 youth with real instrument skills — weekly, hands-on, and built to last.

  • Percussion, keyboards, strings & fretted instruments
  • Weekly structured sessions over 6 months
  • Mentorship & peer-to-peer learning
  • Performance & community showcase opportunities
  • Pathways to teaching and music career mobility
Learn More →
🗺️ Mobile Program
🕊️

Harmony for Peace

A one-day mobile musical peace conference using drumming, storytelling, and guided dialogue to promote healing and unity between communities in Yakurr.

  • Participatory drumming and communal singing
  • Peace dialogue and guided reflection sessions
  • Storytelling and cultural expression
  • Community peace pledge and commitment ceremony
  • Mobile — travels between communities in need

Our Impact

Real change in
real communities

For over 16 years, CCEPI has been a quiet but consistent force for good — from music workshops to digital skills training, always walking beside the communities we serve.

🥁

Participatory Music Learning

Hundreds of youth introduced to musical instruments through immersive, hands-on sessions that build skill and confidence.

🌿

Peacebuilding in Action

Community dialogues powered by music have reduced tension and opened new pathways for reconciliation across settlements and villages.

💻

Python Ugep — Digital Skills

A multi-week software training program that gave young people in Ugep coding skills and a pathway into the technology economy.

📚

Academic Support

Educational assistance and academic enrichment programs that complement our creative offerings and boost long-term outcomes.

👩‍🏫

Youth Leadership

Advanced participants mentor their peers, becoming role models and community leaders who carry the mission forward.

🤝

Community Partnerships

Trusted relationships with local leaders, schools, faith institutions, and cultural groups that amplify our reach and relevance.
